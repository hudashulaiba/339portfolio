Write down deadlines

Whether in a spreadsheet, calendar, or on a random post-it, take note of each school's deadlines for essays, letters of reccommendation, financial aid, and other requirements.

Select Colleges

Look at programs, financial aid, size of the school, etc. to choose where to apply. Focus more on what the school has to offer you as an individual rather than relying on general reputation and sensationalism related to whether or not a school has a "brand name"

Plan essays

Do some research, brainstorming exercises, and prepare outlines to use as references as you flesh out your ideas.

Gather materials

Things like transcripts, lists of your work and activities, test scores, required teacher reccommendations, and any honors or achievements.

Note each school's requirements

Pay attention, different schools have different requirements! Make sure to check what's needed in terms of essays, letters of recommendation, resumes, and extra financial aid requirements.
